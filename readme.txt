This mod requires an Up-To-Date Copy of Freedoom:Phase II to get all of its assets.
This mod requires UZDoom to run.

The Wisperer takes you through a nine-level campaign playing as wisper the wolf, as popularised by the Sonic The Hedgehog series by IDW comics.
